Greetings! Welcome to my homepage!

I am a M.Sc. student in Computer Science at ShanghaiTech University, where I did research in the PLUS Lab with the wonderful Prof. Xuming He. And I got my Bachelor's degree in Data Science at Shandong University, China in 2021.

My past projects cover large vision-language models, visual reasoning, human-object interaction detection and continual learning. My research interests broadly include Computer Vision and Deep Learning, with a focus on Vision-Languge Representation Learning, Continual Learning and Visual Reasoning.
