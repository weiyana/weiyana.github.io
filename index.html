I graduated from Stanford with B.S. (Honors) and M.S. degrees in Computer Science, where I did research in the Stanford Vision and Learning Lab and HCI group with the wonderful Prof. Ranjay Krishna, Prof. Michael Bernstein, and Prof. Fei-Fei Li. I've had research and engineering experience in multi-agent collaboration under reinforcement learning, large language and vision models, and human-computer interaction. My current research interests lie in human-AI interaction and collaboration, vision-language models as well as their compositionality and interpretability.
