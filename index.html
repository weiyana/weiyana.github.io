Greetings! Welcome to my homepage!

I am a first year Ph.D. student in Computer Science at VIU Lab, Johns Hopkins University, advised by Prof. Vishal Patel. I got my M.S. degree at ShanghaiTech University in 2024, supervised by Prof. Xuming He. And I got my Bachelor's degree in Software Engineering at Shandong University in 2021.

My research interests broadly include computer vision, natural language processing and embodied agents, with a focus on vision-languge understanding. I'm excited about creating AI agents that can understand human needs across various modalities.
